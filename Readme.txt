Podcastinator - Podcasts for the procrastinators.

Say you're really busy at work but you have this really interesting ted talk, or article that you want to read, but you can't. AT the same time, you're driving and have all the time in the world, but nothing to do. THe podcastinator is then the solution for you. It creates podcasts out of articles/videos and audios that you can play back whenever you're free!
